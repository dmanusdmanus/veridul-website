# Veridul Product V1

## Purpose

Veridul helps people identify potentially deceptive communications before they lose money, reveal sensitive information, or become victims of fraud.

---

## Core Principle

Veridul should remain silent unless meaningful risk is detected.

False alarms must be minimized.

User trust is the highest priority.

---

## Supported Platform

V1

Android

Future:

* iPhone

V1

---

## Trust Engine

Veridul analyzes multiple signals simultaneously.

No single signal should trigger a warning.

---

## Risk Signals

### Caller Reputation

* Known contact
* Unknown number
* Reported scam number

### Identity Claims

* Family member claim
* Bank claim
* Government claim
* Company claim

### Money Requests

* Transfer request
* Crypto request
* Gift card request
* Payment request

### Urgency

Examples:

* Immediately
* Right now
* Today
* Emergency

### Secrecy

Examples:

* Don't tell anyone
* Keep this secret
* Don't contact the bank

### Verification Codes

Examples:

* Read me the code
* Tell me the SMS code

### Emotional Manipulation

Examples:

* Fear
* Panic
* Pressure
* Threats

### Conversation Context

AI evaluates overall conversation intent.

---

## Warning Levels

### Normal

No warning.

No interruption.

### Verify

Low confidence concern.

Display notification only.

### Stop And Verify

Meaningful risk detected.

Strong visual warning.

### Immediate Verification Required

High confidence dangerous situation.

Strong visual warning.

Vibration alert.

---

## V1 Goal

Detect potentially dangerous conversations while maintaining extremely low false alarm rates.

---

## Success Metric

Users trust Veridul warnings.

Users do not disable or uninstall Veridul due to excessive alerts.

---

## Long-Term Vision

Become the trusted layer for digital communications worldwide.
