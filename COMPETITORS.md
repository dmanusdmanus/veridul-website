# Veridul Competitors

## Purpose

Understand the current market and identify how Veridul differs from existing products.

---

# Truecaller

## What They Do

* Caller identification
* Spam call detection
* Business caller profiles
* Community-based reporting

## Strengths

* Large user base
* Strong caller database
* Well-known brand

## Limitations

* Focuses primarily on phone number reputation
* Does not deeply analyze conversation content
* Limited protection against sophisticated impersonation scams

## Why Veridul Is Different

Veridul focuses on communication trust analysis rather than caller identification alone.

Veridul aims to understand what is being said, not only who is calling.

---

# Hiya

## What They Do

* Spam call detection
* Fraud call blocking
* Caller identification

## Strengths

* Carrier partnerships
* Large spam detection network

## Limitations

* Primarily focused on call reputation
* Limited contextual understanding of conversations

## Why Veridul Is Different

Veridul aims to evaluate communication behavior, intent, urgency, manipulation, and trust indicators during the interaction.

---

# RoboKiller

## What They Do

* Spam call blocking
* Robocall filtering
* Automated answer bots

## Strengths

* Effective robocall protection
* Consumer-friendly experience

## Limitations

* Focused on blocking unwanted calls
* Limited analysis of legitimate-looking scam conversations

## Why Veridul Is Different

Veridul focuses on helping users identify dangerous communications even when the caller appears legitimate.

---

# Google Call Screen

## What They Do

* Screens unknown callers
* Provides call transcripts
* Reduces spam interruptions

## Strengths

* Native Android integration
* Simple user experience

## Limitations

* Primarily designed to screen unknown callers
* Does not provide comprehensive trust analysis

## Why Veridul Is Different

Veridul aims to assess trust and risk throughout the communication, not only before the call is answered.

---

# Norton Genie

## What They Do

* AI-powered scam detection
* Message analysis
* Consumer fraud awareness

## Strengths

* Strong cybersecurity brand
* AI-assisted analysis

## Limitations

* Focused primarily on submitted content
* Not centered around real-time communication protection

## Why Veridul Is Different

Veridul aims to operate during live communications and provide real-time trust guidance.

---

# Market Gap

Most existing solutions focus on:

* Caller identification
* Spam blocking
* Reputation databases
* Post-event analysis

Few solutions focus on:

* Real-time trust assessment
* Communication intent analysis
* Impersonation detection
* Manipulation detection
* Context-aware risk evaluation

---

# Veridul Position

Veridul is not attempting to become another caller ID application.

Veridul aims to become a communication trust platform.

The long-term objective is to help people determine whether a communication may be deceptive before harm occurs.

Supported communications may eventually include:

* Phone calls
* WhatsApp
* Telegram
* SMS
* Email
* Video communications

Mission:

Help people identify potentially deceptive communications before harm occurs.
