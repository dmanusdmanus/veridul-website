# VERIDUL UI DESIGN

Brand Statement

Trust Every Conversation.

Communication is changing. Trust should not.

---

Design Principles

* Calm, not alarming
* Professional, not technical
* Clear, not complicated
* Trust first
* Actionable guidance

---

Primary Colors

Deep Navy
Trust Blue
White

Avoid:

* Hacker green
* Excessive red
* Fear-based design

---

Alert Levels

Normal

No concerns detected.

---

Verification Recommended

Communication contains unusual characteristics.

Proceed carefully.

---

Stop And Verify

Potential deception indicators detected.

Pause and independently verify.

---

Immediate Verification Required

High confidence indicators of deception detected.

Do not proceed until verified.

---

Core Product Screens

1. Monitoring

2. Verification Recommended

3. Stop And Verify

4. Trusted Contact Verification

5. Communication History

6. Settings

7. Family Dashboard

8. Business Dashboard

---

User Experience Goal

Users should feel informed and protected.

They should never feel frightened, overwhelmed, or manipulated by the application.
